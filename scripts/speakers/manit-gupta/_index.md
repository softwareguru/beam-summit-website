Hi everyone, I’m Manit. I’m a Senior Software Engineer at Google on the Cloud Spanner team. Spanner is a multi-modal database that unifies relational, graph, key-value, and search use-cases under a single database, at battle-tested, unlimited scale.
I specialise in building out the infrastructure for heterogeneous database migrations—figuring out the best ways for our customers to move their workload to Spanner.
