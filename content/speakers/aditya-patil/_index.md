Aditya Patil and Raniya Rehman are data engineers at the NJCCIC who combine a background in cybersecurity with modern data engineering practices. They lead the technical design of pipelines that transform billions of raw security events into actionable intelligence. Their work focuses on bridge-building between raw data lakehouses and predictive modeling, ensuring that the state’s defense mechanisms are both scalable and resilient.
