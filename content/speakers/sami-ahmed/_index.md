Sami is a Strategic Solutions Engineer at Confluent specializing in Financial Services (FSI). He leverages tools across the streaming technologies stack to help FSI companies realize their use cases, including realtime fraud detection applications, transactional systems, and bridge to cloud. Previously, he enabled data streaming use cases for small and medium-sized companies in various industries including healthcare, IoT, e-commerce, and aerospace.
