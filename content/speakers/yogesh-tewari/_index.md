Yogesh Tewari is a Senior Cloud Data Engineer at Google, specializing in massive-scale data systems and graph AI. With an M.S. in AI from Johns Hopkins, his expertise spans high-performance genomics data warehousing, GCP Graph databases (Spanner and BigQuery Graph), the GraphFlow GNN toolkit, and applying GNNs to drive Autonomous Network Operations in the telecom industry.
