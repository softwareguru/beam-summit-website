I am a Senior Data Engineer II at Intuit Credit Karma with over 12 years of experience building high-scale data products across GCP, AWS, and on-premise environments. My career has been defined by a passion for delivering robust, data-driven solutions in fast-paced DevOps models. Currently, I lead initiatives focused on streaming platforms and data quality , ensuring the accuracy and timeliness of data that powers critical financial models.

Before joining Intuit, I spent several years at Expedia Group, where I architected real-time attribution platforms using Scala, Kafka, and Cassandra and led large-scale cloud migrations. I am a firm believer in community contribution, having frequently authored technical blogs and mentored emerging engineers. In 2024, I was honored with the Exceptional Impact Program (EIP) award for delivering work that created significant equity value for our shareholders. I hold an M.S. in Computer Science from Indiana University and am a Google Certified Professional Data Engineer.
