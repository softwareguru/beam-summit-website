Jay Jayakumar is a Data Consultant at Google Cloud Professional Services.  He has spent his career immersed in the world of data, building data pipelines, leading data migration projects and designing complex data architectures for customers. 

Jay is committed to helping customers unlock the full value of their data by ensuring it is well-organized, easily accessible, and effectively governed.
