Siddharth Gargava is a Software Development Engineer at Amazon Web Services, where he architects enterprise AI agents and LLM-powered orchestration systems for AWS Marketplace. His work focuses on building production-ready agentic workflows that integrate large language models with existing cloud 
infrastructure at scale.

Siddharth won the NVIDIA AI Hackathon for UnderVolt, a DGX-powered AI platform leveraging NLP and LLMs to surface hidden infrastructure signals. He participated in the Ai4 2025 conference giving a talk on "The Agentic Web," sharing insights on how AI agents will transform digital interactions and micro-
transactions. He has also served as a judge for Northeastern University's AI Tech Challenge, evaluating solutions on responsible innovation and human-centered AI.
