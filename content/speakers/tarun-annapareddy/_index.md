Tarun is a Software Engineer at Google, where he works on Cloud Dataflow, focusing on the evolution of high-throughput, low-latency data processing. As an active contributor to the Apache Beam ecosystem, he is deeply involved in the intersection of unified programming models and distributed execution. Tarun also writes articles in free time on various open-source software developers use daily, such as Postgres, Flink, Temporal etc.,
